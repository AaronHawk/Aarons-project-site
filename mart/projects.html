Swallow Crest Farms

The purpose of this site was to create a nice looking website for a local farm to display their yearly CSA share offerings, and to allow potential customers to get in contact with the owners of the farm. Some of the parameters included an online form, information about a 'pick your own' farm program, and an interactive map.
I used information given by the initiator of the project, Dawn. I used a couple online stock images as well. I used the Avada theme and a host of additional plugins.
